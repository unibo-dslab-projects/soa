Smart Objects and Applications

A Summer School by the University of Bologna. Enrich your knowledge and competence in smart, interconnected objects and their integration in modern applications.

10 Days English 6 CFU

About the Program

Designed for Computer Science and Engineering graduate and undergraduate students, this Summer School bridges theoretical concepts with hands-on practical activities. You will dive deep into the elements at the basis of designing and developing applications based on Smart Objects.

The program is structured across comprehensive classroom lectures and interactive lab sessions, ensuring you gain both the conceptual understanding and the practical skills needed in today's rapidly evolving IoT landscape.

Core Topics

Security of Smart Objects

Explore vulnerabilities and defense mechanisms tailored for IoT ecosystems and interconnected devices.

Human-Smart Object Interaction

Study the paradigms of usability and interaction design when humans interface with autonomous smart systems.

Perception and Control Systems

Understand how objects perceive their environment through sensors and react via advanced control algorithms.

Smart Sensing

Dive into the architecture and data processing techniques underlying modern smart sensor networks.
